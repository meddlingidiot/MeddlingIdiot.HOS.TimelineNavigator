Let me describe the intent of the code. This is a timeline merging tool. It assumes that there are different "feeds" of timestamped data that are coming in (Moments) all are kept within their own segregated list. But then The navigation of the TimelineNavigator should Jumpto() and point on the timeline, and navigate Next and Prior until BeginningOfTime or EndOfTime. Keeping all Moments in cronilogical order with no duplicates at the same timestamp. The StartOfDay timeline is a bit different, it should jump one day at a time but only to the extent of the data included inside the timelines.
